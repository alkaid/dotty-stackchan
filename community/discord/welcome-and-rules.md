# 🤖 Welcome to the Dotty community

Dotty is a self-hosted StackChan voice robot — open firmware, local ASR/TTS, a pluggable LLM brain, and a friendly face for your desk. This server is for building one, hacking on one, and showing off what you made.

**A heads-up:** Dotty is a hobby-grade work-in-progress. It's buggy, it changes day to day, and a fresh deploy on someone else's hardware hasn't been fully verified yet. Bug reports, PRs, and "this didn't work for me" posts are all genuinely welcome here.

## 📜 House rules

We follow the Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct. The short version:

**✅ Please do**
- Be welcoming and inclusive — every experience level belongs here.
- Respect differing viewpoints and accept constructive criticism gracefully.
- Focus on what's best for the community, and show a little empathy.
- Keep posts in a sensible channel so threads stay findable.

**⛔ Please don't**
- Harass anyone, publicly or in DMs.
- Use sexualised language or imagery, or make unwanted advances.
- Troll, insult, or make derogatory, personal, or political attacks.
- Share anyone's private information without their explicit permission.
